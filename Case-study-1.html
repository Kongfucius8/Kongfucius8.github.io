Commercial Sales Performance

Qlik Sense | Snowflake | SQL | CSS Styling
SITUATION
Sales teams were relying on static monthly Excel PDFs to track margins. Data from Domestic and Export markets lived in separate silos, making it impossible to see a consolidated "Global Position" until week's end.
TASK
My objective was to build a live, consolidated Qlik Sense dashboard sourced directly from Snowflake. The tool needed to handle 50M+ rows of transaction data while remaining responsive enough for iPads used by sales reps in the field.
ACTION
I engineered a Snowflake view to unify currency conversions and unit measurements (Lbs vs Kgs). In Qlik, I utilized Set Analysis to create dynamic "Current vs Previous Year" comparisons. I applied custom CSS (Multi-KPI) to style the objects for high readability on smaller screens, moving away from the default grid look.
RESULT
The dashboard reduced reporting latency from 5 days to real-time. Sales managers identified a 4% margin leakage in the Export sector within the first week of use. Adoption rate among the sales team hit 90% within the first month.

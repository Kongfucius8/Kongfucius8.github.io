NPrinting & Automated Reporting

Qlik Sense N-Printing | Documentation
SITUATION
While analysts love dashboards, operational staff and external partners required static, high-fidelity PDFs. Manual compilation of these reports was consuming 10+ hours of analyst time per week and lacked standardization.
TASK
Implement an automated NPrinting solution to distribute complex reports while establishing a governance framework to ensure documentation and maintainability.
ACTION
I designed complex NPrinting templates that aggregated data from multiple Qlik Sense apps. Crucially, I wrote the standard operating procedure (SOP) and technical documentation for the reporting ecosystem, ensuring that any analyst could troubleshoot or update the reports without dependency on a single person.
RESULT
Automated the distribution of daily operational reports to over 100 stakeholders. The accompanying documentation reduced the onboarding time for new team members by providing a clear "Knowledge Base" for the reporting logic.
